About Tutor Log

```

Built by tutors who were tired of keeping every student in their head.

Why we built it

Tutor Log was built by a family of tutors.

We all cared about keeping track of our students properly, but we all had our own slightly chaotic systems.

One of us had Word documents for each student. One relied on weekly WhatsApp messages and had to scroll back through old chats. One used scribbles in a notebook.

It worked, sort of.

But it was messy, easy to lose track of, and different for every tutor.

So we built Tutor Log: something simple enough for all of us to use, no matter how we naturally keep notes.

The bit we actually wanted to solve

The core focus of Tutor Log is simple:

Turn quick tutor scribbles into a useful student history.

During and after a lesson, tutors often have lots of little mental notes:

  • what the student struggled with
  • what score they got
  • what homework was set
  • what needs to be sent later
  • what to pick up next lesson

None of those things are huge on their own.

But across lots of students, they are very easy to lose.

Tutor Log helps turn those quick notes into a tidy timeline, so when the next lesson comes around, you can quickly see what happened last time and what you are working on today.

Not another worksheet machine

We also included a resource library so tutors can quickly access useful websites, books and tools.

But we are not trying to make a fancy worksheet generator.

There are already plenty of good resources out there.

Tutor Log is here to help you remember what you used, what worked, and what each student needs next.

Parent updates without the unpaid evening admin

We also added a parent update feature.

Tutor Log can summarise what happened in the lesson, so you can copy, paste and send a clear update to the parent.

When tutoring is charged by the hour, we believe your work should not keep spilling into your unpaid time.

A quick parent summary helps keep communication clear without taking over your evening.

For all the little things you promise to do later

Finally, we added a to-do list for each student.

If you promised to send homework, need to mark something, chase a parent, print a worksheet or follow up next lesson, you can add it quickly and keep everything in one place.

Nothing too complicated.

Just a simple way to stop those tiny student-specific tasks from disappearing.

What Tutor Log is

Tutor Log is not trying to replace tutors.

It is just here to help tutors stop keeping every student in their head.
```

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI work?

Tutor Log uses advanced Language Models (like GPT-4) securely under the hood. When you submit a raw note, the AI structures it into metrics, action items, and text summaries without altering your original meaning.

Is my student data secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard database security (powered by Supabase). We do not use your notes to train public AI models.

Can I track custom things?

Absolutely. Every student has a "Tracking Rules" setting. You can tell the AI: "Always track Mia's spelling test scores out of 20", and it will pull that specific data out of your messy notes every time.

Do I need to download an app?

No, Tutor Log is a web application. It works beautifully on your laptop browser right after a Zoom lesson, or on your phone browser while you are sitting on the bus.
